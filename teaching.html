Teaching

Inquiry-driven electrochemical education from fundamentals to deployable systems.

Teaching in the Xi Lab emphasizes rigorous fundamentals, active learning, inclusive communication, hands-on experimentation, and translation from scientific principles to engineered devices.

Teaching philosophy

Build confidence through fundamentals, practice, and open communication.

Courses and mentoring will combine thermodynamics, kinetics, transport, materials chemistry, electrochemical characterization, reactor design, techno-economics, and life-cycle thinking. The goal is to train students to ask mechanistic questions and then connect the answers to practical energy, environmental, and chemical-manufacturing systems.
