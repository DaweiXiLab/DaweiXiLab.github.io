Join the lab

We welcome researchers who want to build and study electrochemical systems that matter.

The lab is interested in people with backgrounds in electrochemistry, materials science, chemistry, chemical engineering, mechanical engineering, membrane science, separation process, computation, automation, and characterization.

Prospective PhD students

Apply through the relevant Harvard graduate program. In your email, include your CV, pontential funding sources, research experiences and research interests.

Postdoctoral researchers

Email a CV, a short statement of research interests, pontential funding sources and information of references. Experience with electrochemistry, membranes, separations, or synthesis is especially relevant.

Undergraduate researchers

Email CV and a short note describing your time availability, funding sources, intersts, coursework, and any prior laboratory, coding, design, or characterization experience.
