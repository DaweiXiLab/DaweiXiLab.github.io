Join the lab

We welcome researchers who want to build electrochemical systems that matter.

The lab is interested in people with backgrounds in electrochemistry, materials science, chemistry, chemical engineering, mechanical engineering, membranes, separations, computation, automation, and characterization.

Prospective PhD students

Apply through the relevant Harvard graduate program. In your email, include your CV, research interests, and why the Xi Lab is a good fit.

Postdoctoral researchers

Email a CV, a short statement of research interests, and names of references. Experience with electrochemistry, membranes, separations, or synthesis is especially relevant.

Undergraduate researchers

Email a short note describing your interests, availability, coursework, and any prior laboratory, coding, design, or characterization experience.

Possible projects

Example project areas

  • Electrochemical separations of critical minerals
  • Voltage-gated membranes and conductive nanopores
  • Redox-mediated liquid–liquid extraction
  • Organic diffusion electrodes and nonaqueous electrosynthesis
  • Acid/base electrosynthesis and carbon management
  • Aqueous organic redox flow batteries
  • Operando electrochemical characterization
  • Automated synthesis and high-throughput screening
